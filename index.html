About Me

Over the years, I've spent a lot of time architecting cloud-based, on-premises and hybrid solutions for large corporations all the way down to start-ups. Recently I've helped companies with their AI strategies, platforms and go-to-market execution plans. My philosophy is simple: apply the Lean Start-Up principles to every project I work on. What exactly does that look like? 1. Eliminate uncertainty by testing a vision continuously. Fail fast and fail cheap. 2. Work smarter, not harder. 3. Develop a minimum viable product quickly, measure how customers respond and adjust frequently. 4. Use actionable metrics, milestones and priorities to learn and grow. I've found that this yields quicker results, validates the product meets customer needs more efficiently, and most of all shows faster return on investment to stakeholders. Everyone is happier with faster ROIs.

My Love of AI

I enjoy driving innovation to solve challenging problems for customers with AI and Machine Learning needs. One of my current projects is Open Data Hub, a community-driven open source AI-as-a-service platform for OpenShift, an enterprise distribution of Kubernetes. I also enjoy working with hardware and software vendors to bring their innovative technologies to OpenShift's AI ecosystem.

My Love of Teaching

My other passion is teaching under-served youths about how they can find a career in the software industry. I enjoy showing them the different career paths, connecting them with local entrepreneurs, and working on projects together in a real-world environment.It's only right if I do the same and help youths who don't typically have opportunities to learn about technology.
